The Bay of Puck

The Bay of Puck is one of the most popular places on the Polish coast to go windsurfing. There are many sites around the bay but I recommend villages on the peninsula. There are very good windsurfing conditions, one the one side you have the sea and on the other the bay. You can sail very far from beach in the shallow water.
